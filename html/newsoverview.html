Apex Legends Announces Cross-Play and Expansion to New Platforms

Sony is enhancing its PlayStation esports efforts with a new series of live tournaments called PlayStation Tournaments: XP. The inaugural event will occur on January 18th, 2025, in London, featuring games such as Tekken 8, EA Sports FC 25, Fortnite, and Astro Bot. Players worldwide will compete representing teams named Triangle, Circle, Cross, and Square to earn points and battle for the championship and exclusive prizes. Open qualifiers for Fortnite begin on December 6th. The event will be live-streamed on PlayStation's Twitch and YouTube channels."
